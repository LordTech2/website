Paid Time Off (PTO) and Leave Information

At Lord Technologies, employees are entitled to the following leave types:

  • Paid Time Off (PTO): Up to 40 days per year
  • Vacation: Up to 30 days per year
  • Sick Leave: 25 days per year
  • Other Leave: Special leave requests can be made in advance
