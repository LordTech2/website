Paid Time Off (PTO) and Leave Information

At Lord Technologies, employees are entitled to paid time off (PTO), vacation, and sick leave. The following leave types are available:

  • Paid Time Off (PTO): Up to 10 days per year
  • Vacation: Up to 5 days per year
  • Sick Leave: 3 days per year
  • Other Leave: Special leave requests can be made in advance
